Prospective Students

I am always looking for self-motivated PhD students who are interested in wireless communications & networking, security, and privacy-enhancing techniques. Full financial support in the form of research assistantship (RA) or teaching assistantship (TA) will be offered for qualified students. If interested, please send me your CV, transcript(s), and sample publications if any. Please refer to this link for the GRE/TOEFL requirements and other application-related information.
